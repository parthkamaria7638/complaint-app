Welcome to Complaints App

This WebApp allows citizens to register complaints through webapp or social medias like facebook, twitter and reddit.

Want to post complaints through Social Media? Following are the platforms that we currently support:

Twitter : Direct message or tweet on this account Complaint Receiver

Facebook : Direct message or post on this page Complaint Receiver

Reddit : Post on this subreddit Complaint Receiver

However, for posting complaints through social media, we have a few instructions and rules that may help you to have your complaints registered successfully. You may check them here, it is highly recommended.

Don't use Social Media? Our guest access may be helpful:

Guest : Post your complaints through guest email Complaint Receiver
